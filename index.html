Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

1 Introduction

Juiceverse Arcade ("we", "our", "the app") is a casual fruit-matching mobile game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2 Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide the game experience:

  • Anonymous Device Identifier — A random ID generated on your device used to save your progress. This is not linked to your real identity.
  • Player Name — A name you choose to display on leaderboards. This is not your real name.
  • Game Data — Scores, level progress, XP, avatars, trophies, and in-game currency balances.
  • Push Notification Token — If you opt in to notifications, we store a device token to send you game updates.
  • Friend Code — A short code used to connect with other players in-game.

3 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Real name, email address, phone number, or physical address
  • Location data (GPS, IP-based, or otherwise)
  • Photos, camera, microphone, or contacts access
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID)
  • Cookies, browsing history, or cross-app tracking data
  • Health, financial, or biometric data

4 How We Use Your Data

  • Display your name and scores on public leaderboards
  • Save and sync your game progress across sessions
  • Enable social features (friends list, friend codes)
  • Send push notifications (only if you opt in)
  • Process in-app purchases through Apple App Store or Google Play Store

5 In-App Purchases

All purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not collect, store, or have access to any payment information such as credit card numbers or billing addresses. All payment data is handled entirely by the respective platform.

6 Data Storage & Security

Game data is stored on secure cloud servers (Convex). Local preferences are stored on your device using encrypted storage. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

7 Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with third-party advertisers or analytics providers. The only third-party services used are:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — For in-app purchase processing
  • Expo Push Notifications — For delivering push notifications (if opted in)

8 Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to play. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Play the game without providing any personal information
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time via device settings
  • Set your profile to private to hide from leaderboards
  • Request deletion of your game data by contacting us

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
